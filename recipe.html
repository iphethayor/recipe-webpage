Indomie Noodles

List of Indomie noodles recipe

  • A pack of indomie
  • Shrimps
  • Onions
  • Yellow/Red/Green Pepper
  • Groundnut Oil

Preparation Steps

  1. After Chopping your Onions and Pepper into preffered sizes , proceed and pour your oil and fry it all together for about 3 mins
  2. After per boiling your Indomie noodles , Add it to the fried shrimps and pepper in step 1 and allow them to stir fry for 2mins
  3. Serve as preffered.

Tips and Variation

  1. Do not over fry the Noodles while on step 2 in the Preparation Steps
  2. Use Indomie Ingridents and do not add external cooking spices you have
  3. Always eat hot food to feel the tates
